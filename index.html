About Me

I am a Masters student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of California San Diego, where I work with Prof. Ravi Ramamoorthi. My current research interests lie at the intersection of computer graphics and virtual reality. I obtained the SenseTime Scholarship in 2022.

My first name in Chinese is pronounced very similar to Kevin in English, which is, therefore, my English name. In spare time, I am a huge fan of Cyber Literature (over ten years of experience), and I occasionally play Screeps for fun (you can find my AI here!).
